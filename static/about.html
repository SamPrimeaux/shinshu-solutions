About Jake Waalk

Your local expert with deep roots in Nagano

Shinshu Solutions

Shinshu Solutions is a Nagano-based bilingual consulting service operated by Jake Waalk, specializing in supporting foreign real estate investors and Japanese construction firms.

Service Area

My service area is centered on the world-class ski resort Nozawa Onsen, which was featured on the cover of Condé Nast Traveler as one of the world's top 9 ski resorts for the 2026 season.

Service Areas: Nozawa Onsen, Iiyama, Shinano, Myoko, Karuizawa, and throughout Nagano Prefecture.

Why Choose Shinshu Solutions?

With deep local knowledge and bilingual expertise, I bridge the gap between foreign investors and the Japanese real estate market. Whether you're purchasing property, managing renovations, or need ongoing property care, I provide comprehensive support every step of the way.
