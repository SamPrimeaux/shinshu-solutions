About Jake Waalk

Bridging Cultures in
Nagano's Alps

From the snowy peaks of Nozawa Onsen to the traditional craftsmanship of local industry, I have spent a decade immersing myself in the heart of Japan. I help foreign investors and construction firms succeed by bridging the gap between international vision and local reality.

Follow My Journey

"Living locally isn't just about location—it's about connection."

Deep Roots in Local Soil

I didn't just move to Nagano; I built my life here. Arriving in 2015 with a Master's in Anthropology, I sought to understand the community from the inside out.

My journey took me from teaching English as the President of Nagano AJET to becoming the first non-Japanese Town Development Officer in the prefecture's history, working directly with Tomi City to develop ecotourism.

Whether navigating the nuanced world of the Japanese mushroom export industry or facilitating construction projects, I leverage deep family ties and professional networks to get things done where others hit walls.
